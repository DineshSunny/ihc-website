About Us

Our mission

To create a welcoming space to celebrate the diversity of India through festivals, food, film, music, and meaningful conversation. We connect students, alumni, and the local community.

  • Festival events: Diwali, Holi, Independence Day
  • Workshops: dance, music, cooking, language
  • Community: volunteering & cultural outreach

Quick facts

Membership is open to all students. No fees to join. Leadership opportunities available.
